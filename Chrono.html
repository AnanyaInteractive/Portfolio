Chrono Cleaners

Restore the Forgotten. Clean Through Time.

About the Game

Genre: 2D Hybrid-Casual, Satisfying Cleaning

Platform: iOS, Android (Unity Build)

Studio: Ananya Interactive

Developer: Nitin Kumar

Chrono Cleaners is a peaceful, visually satisfying cleaning simulation where players restore historical, cultural, and mythical objects from different eras. It's a fusion of relaxation, time-travel, and storytelling.

Prototype Highlights

  • 3 fully playable levels: Bench, Clock, Buddha Statue
  • Tools: Pressure Washer, Scrub Brush, Polish Cloth
  • SFX + VFX + Background Music integrated
  • Optimized for CPI testing with Veo-ready ad
